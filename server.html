Existence SMP Community Server


The Community Server is new of Existence SMP, since 2016 our community has grown beyond what anyone of us could have imagined and so the Community Server was opened for a new era of Existence
While it is a Community Server, it will remain whitelisted and is not public. Only select trusted members of the community including legacy members, patrons, and known players will be whitelisted on the server. If you are interested in joining, feel free to make yourself known in our Discord server and have a chat to our members and admins!
The server is running Minecraft 1.17 on PaperMC with a series of plugins including Dynmap!
Visit our Discord and Twitter for the latest news and updates
