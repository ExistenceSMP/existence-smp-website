Existence SMP Community Server


Following the release of Minecraft 1.16, we have opened the Existence Community Server
The Community Server is new home of Existence SMP, since 2016 our community has grown beyond what anyone of us could have imagined and so the Community Server was opened for a new era of Existence

While it is a Community Server, it will remain whitelisted and is not public. Only trusted members of the community including legacy members, patrons, and known players will be whitelisted on the server. If you are interested in joining, feel free to make yourself known in our Discord server and have a chat to our members and admins.

The server is running Minecraft 1.16 vanilla, we plan to run the server on PaperMC when it and our plugins have been updated

Visit our Discord and Twitter for the latest news and updates!

Datapacks
We are running a number of Minecraft Datapacks to enhance gameplay on the server, made by mcpeachpies and Gamemode 4

  • Existence SMP Handler Main server core and join system
    Gameplay features:
    • Fired Spectrual Arrows glow yellow
    • Mobs named "silent" will no emit any sounds or make any noise
      Silenced mobs that are renamed will be unsilenced
  • Multiplayer Sleeping In Development, not yet released
    Gameplay features:
    • Allows players to sleep through the night when 25% of online players (In the overworld) are sleeping
    • Utilises AFK Detector to ignore players who are online but not active
    • Chat message announces require player count to pass night as well as cancel button to kick sleeping players if night is required
  • AFK Detector In Development, not yet released
    Gameplay features:
    • Announces players who have not moved in 15 minutes as away from their keyboards
    • Attaches AFK prefix to players who are away from their keyboards
  • Increased Head Drops In Development, not yet released
    Gameplay features:
    • Player heads will drop when killed by a charged Creeper
      Player heads have a rare chance of being dropped when killed by another player
    • Zombie, Skeleton, and Creeper heads have a rare chance of being dropped when killed by a player
    • Steve heads can be found as rare loot in chests in Dungeons, Woodland mansions, Jungle temples, Bastions, and Strongholds
    • Bonus Feature: Jungle Temple arrows are poisonious
  • Increased Shulker shells In Development, not yet released
    Gameplay features:
    • Adds a chance to receive a second shell from Shulkers
      Chance increased with looting
